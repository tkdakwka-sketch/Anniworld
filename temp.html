FLUX AI MODEL

Pros

- Highly detailed and photorealistic.

- Excellent at expressing subtle elements like lighting, skin texture, and hair.

- Overall, it produces excellent "photorealistic" results.

Cons

- Sometimes feels overly

- Complex background elements (signs, text) may appear distorted or blurred.

▶ Prompt

"A highly detailed anime-style portrait of a young woman standing in a futuristic neon-lit city street at night. She has long flowing hair with blue and purple highlights, wearing a modern cyberpunk outfit with glowing accents. The background shows holographic billboards, flying cars, and rainy reflections on the wet pavement. Cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed textures, photorealistic atmosphere, vibrant colors."

QWEN AI MODEL

Pros

- Relatively well-handled for text insertion, signs, and logos.

- Free/open-source, making it highly accessible and supporting a variety of style conversion and editing features.

- Vivid colors and strong styles, such as cyberpunk.

Cons

- Facial details may be less refined than in Flux.

- Character proportions and hand details can sometimes appear awkward in complex scenes.

▶ Prompt

"A highly detailed anime-style portrait of a young woman standing in a futuristic neon-lit city street at night. She has long flowing hair with blue and purple highlights, wearing a modern cyberpunk outfit with glowing accents. The background shows holographic billboards, flying cars, and rainy reflections on the wet pavement. Cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed textures, photorealistic atmosphere, vibrant colors."

GEMINI AI MODEL

Pros

- Maintains story consistency and the ability to seamlessly create multiple scenes.

- Integrated with the Google ecosystem, providing easy accessibility, and powerful multimodal functionality.

Cons

- Limited creative and character style control

→ Less effective at defining a specific artistic tone than Flux.

- difficult to strongly reflect detailed artistic styles (e.g., a specific painting style, extreme emphasis on detail) like Flux or Stable Diffusion.

She has long flowing hair with blue and purple highlights, wearing a modern cyberpunk outfit with glowing accents. The background shows holographic billboards, flying cars, and rainy reflections on the wet pavement. Cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed textures, photorealistic atmosphere, vibrant colors."

Integration of AI

I2I & T2I

- Specific and hierarchical prompts: The model will respond more accurately if you write them in the order of topic → style → mood → detail.

- Repeat sampling: It is efficient to try the same prompt multiple times and select the result you like best.
